Our background

Gold Craft Jewellery is experienced in the business and trade world since 1950 AD by the establishment of Gold Craft Jewellery gold and jewelry factory and a series of retail branches. The beginning was with gold and later in jewelries and precious stones. As an extension of this deep-rooted history and based on it, we expanded first from the Arab Gulf, via the Middle East, through Gold Craft Jewellery Gold & Jewelry Est., Gold Craft Jewellery gold factory and Fancy Rose Company to reach today Europe and USA by having partnerships with a number of worldwide companies in various business and industrial sectors. Gold Craft Jewellery Gold & Jewelry Est., Gold Craft Jewellery gold factory and Fancy Rose Company have assigned and designed a number of strategic goals in a bid to contribute to the accomplishment of its vision for its subordinate companies. Gold Craft Jewellery Holding Company provides financial and administrative support to a group of companies and supervises the budget and cash liquidity. Gold Craft Jewellery Gold & Jewelry Est., Gold Craft Jewellery gold factory and Fancy Rose Company are comprised of qualified professional cadres. Success is their characteristic, gathered by Gold Craft Jewellery trademark combined with the honorable lineage of the past... the success of the present... and enlightened future.

Chairman's Message

We live in a modern world, in which cultural barriers and borders are not only transparent, but also open to each other. Technology has allowed us to travel anywhere on the planet and communicate with anyone of our own free will. For many years, my personal view of the world was reflected in the philosophy expressed by Alfred de Vigny, the famous French poet of the 19th century who said: "History is the novel...the people are the author." There is no doubt that inherited traditions are part of culture and culture is the natural reflection of the civilization of every society. Therefore, the civilization of societies and countries is measured by the development and richness of their culture, in line with the spirit of modern values. We have many expectations, great ambitions, and love and loyalty to the profession that we inherited from our ancestors. The real challenge lies in the great responsibility that has been placed upon us, and we believe that our true duty is to enrich the heritage of our ancestors, in order to embody honesty in its modernity, while preserving the core values, and passing them on to our future generations. We are sure that customers' satisfaction is the key to success, and this is not easy, so customers' trust and satisfaction are our most valuable assets.
